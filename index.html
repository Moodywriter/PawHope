Our Mission

Many stray dogs and cats struggle to find food daily. PawHope addresses this problem by installing automatic food dispensers in critical areas where stray populations are high.


Our business model funds these dispensers through the sale of pet products such as treats and collars. 35% of profits are reinvested directly into building and installing new feeding stations.

Real Impact

Each PawHope feeding dispenser provides consistent food access for stray animals that would otherwise struggle to survive. With every product sold, we move closer to installing more feeding stations in communities that need them most.
