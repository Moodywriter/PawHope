SDG 15

Life on Land

The United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 15 focuses on protecting ecosystems, biodiversity, and animal life. It encourages countries, organizations, and communities to promote sustainable interaction with nature and protect living species.

How PawHope Supports SDG 15

Millions of stray dogs and cats live without reliable access to food and water. While they are often overlooked, they are part of the ecosystems and communities where humans live.

PawHope contributes to SDG 15 by installing automatic feeding dispensers in areas with large stray animal populations. These dispensers help animals survive while reducing suffering and promoting humane treatment of animals.

Through the sale of pet products such as treats and collars, PawHope reinvests 35% of its profits into building and installing these feeding stations, creating a sustainable system that continuously supports stray animals.

Why This Matters

Protecting animals is not only an ethical responsibility but also contributes to healthier ecosystems and communities. Sustainable and humane treatment of animals helps build more responsible societies.

By supporting PawHope, customers contribute to a project aligned with the United Nations global effort to protect life on land.
